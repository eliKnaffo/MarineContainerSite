About Marine Container

Marine Containers is specialized in providing empty containers service for shipping companies and ship agents.
The service includes: gate check,receiving,cleaning and storage until the container is delivered to the client.
Marin Container was and still am a pioneer at keeping the highest standards At 1986 was the first off its kind to have an ISO9000 standard.
The workers at Marine Container are trained by Israeli and International standard of work with Cooling Containers and as a CARRIER facility.
Part of the Marine Containers Facility is the industrial train this allows the company to move containers from Ashdod to Haifa for storage and
export purposes
